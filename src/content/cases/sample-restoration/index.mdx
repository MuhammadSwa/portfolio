## Technique Details

1. **Isolation**: Rubber dam applied for optimal moisture control
2. **Cavity preparation**: Minimal intervention approach
3. **Matrix placement**: Tofflemire system with wooden wedge
4. **Bonding**: Total-etch technique with 15-second etch time
5. **Layering**: Incremental technique with 2mm layers
6. **Finishing**: Fine diamond burs followed by polishing discs

## Outcome

Patient reported immediate relief of symptoms. The restoration demonstrated excellent marginal adaptation and anatomical contour.
